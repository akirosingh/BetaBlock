Privacy Policy for BetaBlock Chrome Extension

Last Updated: 12/2/2023

Introduction
This Privacy Policy applies to the BetaBlock Chrome extension. Our extension is designed to censor beta information on 8a.nu and Mountain Project to preserve the integrity of onsight climbing attempts. This document outlines how we handle any data we might collect from our users.

Information We Collect
The BetaBlock extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not track or monitor our users' browsing activities. Our extension operates exclusively within the client's browser without any interaction with external servers or services, except for those provided by the Chrome Web Store.

How We Use Information
Since our extension does not collect personal data, there is no usage of such data. All preferences and settings related to the extension’s functionality are stored locally on the user's device and are not accessible to us.

Data Storage
The BetaBlock extension stores user settings and preferences locally on the user's device using Chrome’s local storage API. This information is solely to maintain user preferences for the extension’s functionality and is not shared or transmitted.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the “Last Updated” date.
